# Marketplace

Version: 0.1

Status: Living Document

---

# Overview

Lewis OS is more than a venture preparation service.

Its long-term vision includes a marketplace where structured ideas become discoverable opportunities.

Instead of publishing unfinished concepts, the marketplace publishes venture-ready projects prepared through the Lewis OS methodology.

---

# Why a Marketplace?

Thousands of ideas never move forward.

Some founders lack:

- technical skills
- funding
- time
- partners
- confidence

Others have the ability to build but lack high-quality opportunities.

Lewis OS aims to reduce this gap.

---

# Marketplace Participants

## Founders

Submit ideas and transform them into structured ventures.

## Builders

Discover projects that are ready for execution.

## Investors

Explore opportunities that already include documentation, roadmap, business model, and budget estimation.

## Advisors

Improve projects before they move into development.

---

# Project Levels

Projects may appear in different maturity stages.

### Concept

A validated opportunity with structured documentation.

### Blueprint

A complete project architecture ready for development planning.

### Prototype

An initial demonstration of the idea.

### MVP

A usable first product.

### Growth

Projects seeking partners, customers, or investment.

---

# Marketplace Rules

Every published project must include:

- Clear ownership
- Project status
- Documentation
- Roadmap
- Version history
- Licensing information
- Contact method

Transparency creates trust.

---

# Lewis OS Role

Lewis OS does not automatically become the owner of projects.

Lewis OS provides structure, documentation, and preparation.

Ownership always follows explicit agreements.

---

# Future Vision

The marketplace should become a trusted library of venture-ready opportunities.

Ideas become assets.

Knowledge becomes reusable.

Preparation becomes visible.
