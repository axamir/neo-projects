# Pricing Philosophy

Lewis OS begins with a simple idea:

A founder should not need a large budget to understand whether an idea deserves to move forward.

The first three services are intentionally priced at **$96.3 each**.

The goal is not to sell expensive uncertainty.

The goal is to create clear, useful, and independently valuable outcomes at every step.

# Delivery Priority

The standard price applies to the standard delivery queue.

Faster delivery requires additional capacity and receives a priority multiplier.

| Delivery | Target Time | Multiplier |
|---|---:|---:|
| Standard | Up to 30 days | ×1 |
| Priority | Up to 15 days | ×2 |
| Express | Up to 8 days | ×4 |
| Critical | Up to 4 days | ×8 |

The multiplier purchases priority and compressed delivery time.

It does not change the agreed scope or quality standard.

All timelines remain subject to project complexity, client response time, and confirmed capacity.

---

# Scope Protection

Each $96.3 stage covers:

- one primary idea
- one initial target market
- one agreed deliverable set
- one structured review cycle
- reasonable corrections within the approved scope

Additional markets, products, major pivots, or repeated revision cycles require a separate scope.
